The Fall of Lazarus

Techonology: Unreal Engine 4

The Fall of Lazarus was my first commercial game developed during my time at No Wand Studios. I took the roles of technical director, producer and programmer. As a producer I was in charge of manage the team and take care of all the development cost and planification. As a programmer, during the first version my work was to take care of all the UI elements of the game and engine related issues. For the second version I worked in almost every piece of the game, from gameplay, to lightning or optimization.
